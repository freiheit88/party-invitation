The square as a prelude

We begin outside Alte Oper. Not indoors, not in a rush. Just standing on the square while the city keeps moving around us.

People will arrive quietly between 19:30 and 20:00. You can take your time. The first A will be soft, almost shy. Later, when the square feels ready, we will move together.

How to start

Come to Alte Oper with enough time to let your own pace slow down.

Stand on the square for a moment. Notice the traffic, the footsteps, the small conversations folding into each other. If you let it, the whole place starts to breathe at the same tempo.

When you are ready, let your A ring once. The app will stay quiet enough not to disturb the square, but present enough that you feel it.
